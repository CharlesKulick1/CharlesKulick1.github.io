2024 DRP Timeline

Check out the tentative schedule of 2024 events! Exact dates will be updated as the program runs.

Timeline:

September 14:
Mentor applications go live!

October 14:
Mentor applications are due.

October 31: 
Mentee application forms go live! 

November 2:
Meet the mentors event!

November 30: 
DRP kickoff event for both applicants and mentors! 

November 18: 
Mentee applications are due. 

Late November/Early December: 
Mentors and Mentees will be notified of their DRP participation status. 

Mid-December: 
A meeting to discuss the DRP community agreement, and for mentors and mentees to meet each other and discuss possible reading topics and agree on a weekly meeting time.

March Career Panel: 
This panel is comprised of recent UCSB graduates, professors, and knowledgeable sources to discuss paths forward for careers in mathematics. 

3 Week Between-Quarter Break 

April Presenting Mathematics: Making and Presenting a Poster
Join us for a workshop discussing how to make a poster that effectively communicates the mathematics you've engaged in this year.

Spring Quarter, Week 8 
Poster submissions due! 

Early June Poster Session - End of Spring Quarter 
The poster session will be held on campus. We will have light refreshments and some pizza. All are welcome! 

After the Poster Session
All DRP participants will receive surveys to assess the program.

Typically held in South Hall 6635, our panel events are small open floor panels where speakers can share their experience and answer questions dynamically. Our panelists are highly knowledgable on the topic at hand, and have historically come from the UCSB graduate student body, UCSB professors, and UCSB alumni.
